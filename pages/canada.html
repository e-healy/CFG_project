About Canada

As the second largest country in the world, there is no shortage of things to do throughout Canada. From the rocky mountains of British Columbia to the bustling city life of Toronto, there is so much to see. Whether you're vising for a few weeks or just passing through, dont miss out on these must see's!


Top things to see in Canada:


1. Toronto

Toronto is the home of the CN tower which provides beautifulpanoramic views of the city. If you're feeling brave, once at the top you can walk across the glass floor or take it a step further and walk on the Edge Walk- a ledge that circles the tower and see the city unrestricted from above.

2. Niagara Falls

You can't go all the way to Canada and not see Niagara falls! whether its summer or winter, the falls are incredibly beautiful all year round. It's also a great opportunity to experience Niagara on the Lake, a small village which will make you feel like you've stepped into a hallmark movie. There's great food options and you can also take a boat trip in the middle of the lake! PS it is also way better then the New York side!

3. The snow!

Canada wouldnt be Canada without snow! With so many of its cities being covered with snow, it's no wonder that Canadians love their winter sports. Why not check out the Ice rink in the middle of Toronto, or experice the beauty of Niagara when frozen over. Whether you're adventurous and love skiing or would prefer to sit in a stadium and watch an ice hockey game, Canada offers a surplus of activiies no matter the weather.
